Our Tools

We are offering a set of complementing tools that can be freely combined to tokenize shares and create a market for them. The legal foundations for all of this will come into force on February 1st, 2021.

Corporate Dashboard

A website where board members of tokenized companies can manage the shareholder registry, initiate and approve transactions, and configure the smart contracts that belong to the company.

Visit
Portfolio App

A mobile wallet app to track and manage investments in Ethereum-based assets as well as positions in traditional shares of partner companies. Supports the WalletConnect protocol.

Market

A smart contract for the automated market-making of tokenized securities. It is tailored towards the needs of issuers of equity and backed by scientific research. Delivered as a web-based widget.

Tokenization

Tokenize existing or newly issued shares of your company by minting blockchain-based security tokens that implement the popular ERC-20 standard and follow the Security Token Guidelines published by the Swiss Blockchain Federation.

Shareholder Registry

Shareholders must register themselves with name and address in order to enjoy their rights. We provide an according registration widget and maintain the shareholder registry in an exportable format.

Documentation
Drag-Along Contract

A smart contract to automatically enforce the drag-along clause typically found in shareholder agreements, enabling a blockchain-driven squeeze-out in case of an acquisition.

Documentation
Token Recovery

Tokens issued with us include a decentralized token recovery mechanism that allows lost tokens to be returned to their owners.

Documentation
Collective Signatures

A user-friendly multisignature contract to securely perform corporate actions, supporting the typical signatory setups of Swiss corporations.

Documentation
Legal Package

Comprehensive consultation as well as a legal package including articles of association, token terms, and a revision of the shareholder agreement is provided by our partner and law firm lexr.ch.
