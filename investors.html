Business Model

Markets work best when they are free and open. We leverage the latest legal and technical developments to create liquid markets for securities that hitherto were illiquid and hard to price. Even for those not interested in selling their shares, having a reliable price signal can unlock substantial value. For example, it creates a point of orientation for employees regarding the true value of their shares and enables investors to more reliably gauge how well (or not so well) their portfolio is doing.

We believe that being enabler in this market should put us sooner or later into a position where we can capture some of the value we create. However, for the foreseeable future, we strictly prioritize growth and product development over revenue generation. We also decided to put a laser-sharp focus on the technical dimension, leaving legal consulting to partner firm LEXR and the provision of auxiliary financial services such as the custody of crypto assets to regulated financial intermediaries. For the technically savvy, it will always be possible to enjoy the full spectrum of our services without relying on third parties, but some users find it more convenient to do so via an intermediary of choice.

Financials

Aktionariat AG has been founded in June 2020 and has not completed any accounting year yet. At the end of 2020, we had about 30'000 CHF liquidity left in our bank accounts and only minimal income. More details to be published soon. We do not expect to reach break-even in the coming years and therefore depend on further investments. These can come from three sources: the founders, employees using part of their salaries to buy more shares, and external investors that are adventerous enough to believe in our vision. Our aim is to route all of these transactions through our open market widget, giving every buyer the exact same opportunity to buy our shares and creating a high level of market transparency.

Potential investors should note that unlike Bitcoin, shares of a company do not have a limited supply. The majority of the shareholders can always decide to print and offer more shares to finance the company's goals (if they finds a buyer). We plan to make extensive use of this possibility, leading to significant dilution over the coming years. However, unlike with Bitcoin, every shareholder always gets the right to buy his fair share of newly issued shares by law. This is one of the advantages of building on proven concepts and issuing another crypto currency: shares come with a bundle of legally enshrined and time-tested rights that protect the shareholders.

Market

In our vision, a direct market is the central component of every investor relations page, enabling investors to buy and sell shares directly from the company. In this case, we offer Aktionariat AG Shares (DAKS). These blockchain-based security tokens represent shares of Aktionariat AG that are bound to a Token Holder Agreement. The token holder agreement preserves the strategic option of an exit by allowing an acquirer to squeeze out all minority shareholders in case a 75% majority agrees with the acquisition. This is usually called a "drag-along agreement", hence the prefix "Draggable" in the token name. The DAKS token contract allows the drag-along agreement to be automatically enforced, allowing it to be efficiently executed even if there are thousands of shareholders. At the same time, the agreement also protects the minority token holders by ensuring that they can sell their shares at the same price as the big shareholders in such an event ("tag-along").

Investors can use the Aktionariat mobile app for iOS or Android , MetaMask or any other WalletConnect compatible Ethereum wallet to interact with the market widget and complete their transactions without any need for intermediaries. With each sold share, the price is automatically adjusted upwards a little and with each repurchased shares it is adjusted downwards again by the same amount, allowing a market price to be formed.

The market widget is powered by an open-source smart contract and does depend on Aktionariat's servers to function.
