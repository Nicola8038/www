Investor Relations

General

Aktionariat AG is a Swiss corporation based in Zurich with trade register number ZEFIX-LINK. Its purpose is to enable Swiss companies to create a market for their shares. Its capital is divided into TOTAL-SUPPLY shares, out of which TOKENIZED-SHARES have been issued as blockchain-based ERC-20 tokens with the ticker symbol TICKER. TREASURY shares are held by the company itself.

We will start to tokenize our shares using our own technology as soon as the Federal Council sets the Swiss "blockchain law" into force, which we hope to happen by summer 2021. In the meantime, feel free to play with our test tokens with symbol TAKT. You can trade them directly on this website using our Automaton and a WalletConnect-compatible wallet. Regardless of which wallet you prefer to manage your private keys, we recommend to install the Aktionariat app to monitor your portfolio of crypto assets as it is the only wallet that is aware of the additional data available for our share tokens.

Business Model

Markets work best when they are free and open. We wnat to make use of the latest legal end technical developments to create open and liquid markets for securities that hitherto were illiquid and hard to price. Even without trading, having a reliable price indication can unlock substantial value. For example, it creates a credible signal for employees regarding the true value of shares and allows investors to more reliably appreciate how well (or not so well) their portfolio is doing.

We believe that being enabler in this market should put us sooner or later into a position where we can capture some of the value we create. However, for the foreseeable future, we strictly prioritize growth and product development over revenue generation. We also decided to put a laser-sharp focus on the technical dimension, leaving legal consulting to partner firm LEXR and the provision of auxiliary financial services such as the custody of crypto assets to regulated financial intermediary. For the technically savvy, it will always be possible to enjoy the full spectrum of our services, but some users will always prefer doing so through an intermediary of choice.

We use the services of Aktionariat to automatically keep our shareholder registry up to date. By submitting your data you agree with Aktionariat processing your data and automatically registering yourself as a shareholder for all share tokens held on the registered address (now and in the future, and potentially also for tokens of other issuers). In case you only want to be registered for specific shares, please get in touch with us. More details can be found in the share transfer documentation as well as the privacy policy of Aktionariat. By default, Aktionariat will also use your email address for important notifications such as an attacker trying to abuse the token recovery feature to get hold of your shares.
