Investor Relations

This page serves as our own investor relations page as well as an example for other issuers using our platform.

All charts, tables, forms, and other widgets on this page are freely configurable, allowing issuers to easily create their own investor relations page for their own token. Data points like the number of shares in circulation are fetched from the blockchain as the page is loaded, ensuring that they stay up to date without manual intervention.

General

Aktionariat AG is a Swiss corporation based in Zurich with trade register number CHE-453.438.437. Its purpose is to enable Swiss companies to create a market for their shares.

We will start to tokenize our shares using our own technology as soon as the Federal Council sets the Swiss "blockchain law" into force, which should happen some time in 2021. Business Model

Markets work best when they are free and open. We leverage the latest legal and technical developments to create liquid markets for securities that hitherto were illiquid and hard to price. Even for those not interested in selling their shares, having a reliable price signal can unlock substantial value. For example, it creates a point of orientation for employees regarding the true value of their shares and enables investors to more reliably gauge how well (or not so well) their portfolio is doing.

We believe that being enabler in this market should put us sooner or later into a position where we can capture some of the value we create. However, for the foreseeable future, we strictly prioritize growth and product development over revenue generation. We also decided to put a laser-sharp focus on the technical dimension, leaving legal consulting to partner firm LEXR and the provision of auxiliary financial services such as the custody of crypto assets to regulated financial intermediaries. For the technically savvy, it will always be possible to enjoy the full spectrum of our services without relying on third parties, but some users find it more convenient to do so via an intermediary of choice.

Market

The market is the central component of the investor relations page, enabling investors to buy and sell shares directly from the company.

Investors can use the Aktionariat mobile app, MetaMask or any other WalletConnect compatible Ethereum wallet to interact with the market widget and complete their transactions without any need for intermediaries.

The market widget is powered by a open-source smart contract and does depend on Aktionariat's servers to function.

Note that our own market is distributing only Test Aktionariat AG Shares (DAKT) for the time being.

Trade History

The trade history widget shows the most recent share transactions. It is automatically updated by on-chain data. The complete trade history is also available for download as a csv file.
