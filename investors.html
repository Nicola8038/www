General

Aktionariat AG is a Swiss corporation based in Zurich with trade register number CHE-453.438.437. Its purpose is to enable Swiss companies to create a market for their shares.

We will start to tokenize our shares using our own technology as soon as the Federal Council sets the Swiss "blockchain law" into force, which should happen some time in 2021. In the meantime, feel free to play with our test token. There are test tokens in circulation, out of which have been issued as blockchain-based ERC-20 named . tokens are held by the company itself. Business Model

Markets work best when they are free and open. We leverage the latest legal and technical developments to create liquid markets for securities that hitherto were illiquid and hard to price. Even for those not interested in selling their shares, having a reliable price signal can unlock substantial value. For example, it creates a point of orientation for employees regarding the true value of their shares and enables investors to more reliably gauge how well (or not so well) their portfolio is doing.

We believe that being enabler in this market should put us sooner or later into a position where we can capture some of the value we create. However, for the foreseeable future, we strictly prioritize growth and product development over revenue generation. We also decided to put a laser-sharp focus on the technical dimension, leaving legal consulting to partner firm LEXR and the provision of auxiliary financial services such as the custody of crypto assets to regulated financial intermediaries. For the technically savvy, it will always be possible to enjoy the full spectrum of our services without relying on third parties, but some users find it more convenient to do so via an intermediary of choice.
